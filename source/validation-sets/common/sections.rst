sections

maximum representation of six (6) levels. Below will use the valid
`reStructuredText section`_ characters to represent these header levels.

section title
'''''''''''''

section title
`````````````

section title

section title
*************

section title
+++++++++++++

section title

section title

section title

section title
%%%%%%%%%%%%%

section title

section title
(((((((((((((

section title
)))))))))))))

section title
